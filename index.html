Our STORY

After our worthless studies graduation, two of us decided to open cozy burger bar. It was hard but quickly grows bigger (that what she said). Once some guy asked us why not transform our business to food truck? He had driving licence so we took him to our gang and started our journey...
... and here we are, 2019, three musketeers, hunger for attention, travelling from city to city.
Great adventure, many friends and best burgers in Poland!
Our RECIPE is simple...
  • 100% best quality beef 80/20 meat/fat proportion to make it freaking juicy
  • white flour's buns full of gluten + 7 sesame seeds (seriously... always 7 there are, no more, no less)
  • local and seasonal vegetables full of ground, but don't worry - we wash them... do we?
  • special magic burger sauce don't ask... we wont tell you anyway but it'll blow your mind
